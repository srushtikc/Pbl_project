Yoga Poses to Relieve Stress

Child's Pose (Balasana)

This gentle resting pose helps stretch the lower back and hips, promoting relaxation. It calms the mind, relieving tension and stress.

Cat-Cow Pose (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana)

This is a dynamic flow between two poses that warms the body and brings flexibility to the spine. It helps to relieve stress by synchronizing movement with breath.

Standing Forward Bend (Uttanasana)

Standing Forward Bend stretches the hamstrings and back, releasing tension in the neck and shoulders. It has a calming effect on the mind and reduces anxiety.

Legs Up the Wall (Viparita Karani)..

This gentle inversion allows the mind and body to relax. It helps reduce fatigue and stress, while promoting blood circulation and calming the nervous system.

Corpse Pose (Savasana)

Often practiced at the end of a yoga session, Savasana allows complete relaxation of the body and mind, relieving tension and stress.
